# Diagram 02: Core Research Areas

## Purpose
Depict the research pillars that guide implementation and evaluation.

## Required clusters
1) Evidence & Provenance
- content-addressed artifacts
- anchoring/spans
- lineage + correction workflows

2) Accessibility & Inclusion
- transcripts/captions/alt modalities
- accommodations hooks (IEP/504/gifted)
- multimodal learning support

3) Jurisdictional Policy & Standards
- JurisdictionProfile
- standards ID grammar (STD::*)
- compliance parameter sheets

4) Pedagogy & Didactics
- Montessori parameters (mastery/self-paced)
- Waldorf parameters (epoch/rhythm/narrative)
- hybrid profile support

5) Decisioning & NBA
- constraint-first optimization
- explainability + justification artifacts
- omnichannel renderers

6) Governance & Community
- attribution/provenance under MIT
- review roles
- reversible change (ChangeSets)

## Style constraints
- No sensitive inputs, no copied labels.
- Use our own taxonomy and improved naming.
